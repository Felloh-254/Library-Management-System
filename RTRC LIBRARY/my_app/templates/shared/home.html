Welcome to Rarieda Training And Resource Center Library

We are registered with Kenya Library Association(KLA), both an academic and community library, situated at Rafiki Wa Maendeleo Trust, Lusi village in Rarieda Sub-county.

We boast of a well-developed collection of books and other information resources that support the research, innovation, and academic excellence of our Users.

We offer a good spacious reading environment, internet access, and well-sorted references for our diverse users.

RTRCL(Rarieda Training And Resource Center Library)

Our Goal and Aim is to;

  1. Promote Literacy in Rarieda Sub-county by encouraging a Love for Reading for RTRC students and the Rarieda Community.
  2. Support Education by offering Resources and Services that Complement Curriculum in the RTRC College and the partner Schools in Rarieda sub county.
  3. Community Engagement and Digital Literacy by facilitating digital skills and engaging the community and facilitating social interaction at the Library for members to connect, interact, and collaborate

Our Objectives as a Department is to;

  1. Expand our Collection to include Local Content books to facilitate Cultural Diversion.
  2. Collaborate with Educational Institutions to support Literacy works and academic improvements within Rarieda.
  3. Enhance Facilities and services to conform with current trends in Library world.

Our Operating time is between 8am to 5:30 pm Monday to Friday.

We are proud of our donors who supplies us with Yearly Book donations + other Library supplies, American Friends of Kenya (AFK).

RTRC(Rarieda Training And Resource Center)

Our coursework is based on Experiential Learning, or learning by doing. Students are able to quickly apply what they have learned in the classroom – 80% of their time is spent in workshops for practical training.

RESULTS

A by-product of students gaining immediate practical experience is the availability of products (produce, fashion & design, carpentry, etc.) and services (hair & beauty, ploughing, water delivery, etc.) to the general public.

Mission & Vision
