Support Page

Borrowing Policies

  • Loan Period: Books can be borrowed for 14 days.
  • Renewals: You can renew your books up to 2 times.
  • Overdue Fees: Late fees are Kshs 10 per day per item.

Reserving Books

To reserve a book that is currently checked out, please visit our online catalog or contact the library staff.

Digital Resources

  • E-books: Access our collection of e-books through our online catalog.
  • Online Databases: Explore our databases for research and academic resources.
  • Remote Access: Most of our digital resources can be accessed remotely with your library account.
